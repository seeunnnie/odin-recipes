Lasagna

A delicious pasta dish filled with cheese, pasta sauce, and meat! Baked in the oven at the perfect temperature and time, it has a crispy top and warm flavor.

This Italian dish can be made in a vegetarian or non-vegetarian way. The classic meat put into a lasagna is italian sausage along with ricotta cheese. You can also top it off with some fresh herbs!

Ingredients for 15 servings
  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 1 pound ground chuck 80/20
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 28 ounces crushed tomatoes
  • 12 ounces tomato paste
  • 15 ounces tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup freshly chopped basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 15 ounces whole milk ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • dash nutmeg
  • 1 pound whole milk mozzarella shredded
  • 9-10 slices of provolone cheese
  • 1/2 cup freshly grated romano cheese
  • 1/2 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese

Instructions!

  1. Use a deep dish lasagna pan or a deep 9x13" baking dish
  2. In a large saucepan, cook the sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic until cooked through. Drain the fat
  3. Add the tomatoes, paste, sauce, water, sugar, basil, fennel, Italian seasoning, salt, pepper, and parsley. Stir well
  4. Simmer, covered, over low heat for 1 1/2 hours, stirring occassionally
  5. Place the lasagna noodles in a pan of hot water and make certain they are completely covered. Allow them to sit for 30 minutes to soften, then drain the water.
  6. In a small bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese, egg and nutmeg. In a different small bowl, mix together the Parmesan and Romano cheeses.

Now time to put together the Lasagna!!

  1. Spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce on the bottom of the lasagna pan.
  2. Place 4 lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  3. Spoon half of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
  4. Top with 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese.
  5. Spread another 1 1/2 cups of sauce over the mozzarella, then sprinkle with 1/3 cup of the Parmesan mixture.
  6. Lay another 4 noodles over the cheese.
  7. Top with the remaining ricotta mixture.
  8. Lay 9-10 slices of provolone cheese over the ricotta, then top with 1 1/2 cups meat sauce.
  9. Sprinkle 1/3 cup Parmesan mixture over the sauce.
  10. Top with 4 more noodles, the remaining sauce, 2 cups of mozzarella and the remaining Parmesan mixture.
  11. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F. Bake immediately for 25 minutes, covered with foil, and then an additional 30 minutes with the foil removed
  12. Allow the lasanga to cool for 15 minutes before slicing
  13. Store any leftovers in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 4 days.
